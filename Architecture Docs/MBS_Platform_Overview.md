# Magic Bus Studios — Platform Overview

**Date**: March 2026

---

## What Is Magic Bus Studios?

Magic Bus Studios is a digital product studio that builds AI-powered experiences for personal growth, entertainment, and everyday productivity. All products live under one roof — one account gets you access to everything.

The studio operates three product lines:

- **Inner Lab** — A unified system for inner growth, featuring 11 interconnected modules that learn about you over time and personalize their guidance.
- **The Arcade** — Five AI-powered multiplayer games designed for parties, classrooms, and friend groups.
- **Studio Works** — Six AI-powered tools for cooking, shopping, learning, wildlife identification, movie discovery, and family task management.

## The Inner Lab

Inner Lab is the heart of Magic Bus Studios — a collection of 11 modules that work together to support personal growth, self-discovery, and wellness.

What makes Inner Lab different from a collection of separate apps is the **shared intelligence layer**. With your permission, insights from one module can enhance another. A meditation pattern noticed by FlowState might inform the guidance you receive in Conversations With God. A dream logged in DreamLens might connect to themes explored in your Arcana readings.

### Active Modules

**Conversations With God** — AI-powered spiritual guidance through conversations with 21 guides from diverse wisdom traditions. Features journaling, daily wisdom, sacred text exploration, and community reflections.

**FlowState (MindBody Flow)** — AI yoga, breathwork, and meditation companion with personalized practice sequences, guided programs, and progress tracking.

### Coming Soon

BreathArc (guided breathwork), StarMap (astrology charts), AstroCompass (astrological navigation), Arcana (tarot readings), Archetypes (personality archetypes), DreamLens (dream journaling and interpretation), Rituals (daily ritual builder), InnerQuest (guided self-discovery quests), and Nexus (cross-module intelligence hub).

### How Inner Lab Modules Connect

Each module can contribute to a shared understanding of you:
- **Consciousness Profile** — Your spiritual archetype and orientation
- **Personal History** — Your life story, shared with AI guides for personalized responses
- **Check-Ins** — Daily mood, energy, and stress tracking from any module
- **Memories** — Facts the AI learns about you, shared across modules only with your explicit permission
- **Wellness Profile** — Health conditions, injuries, and goals (for yoga/breathwork safety)

Privacy is central to Inner Lab's design. All cross-module data sharing is opt-in. By default, what happens in one module stays in that module.

## The Arcade

Five AI-powered multiplayer games, each designed to be picked up in minutes and played with friends:

**Broken Chain** — A collaborative storytelling game where players build chains of narrative, and AI judges the creativity and coherence of each link.

**MindHacker** — A social deduction game set in a cyberpunk lab. Players take on roles and use AI-generated clues to identify the hidden hacker among them.

**AI Trivia Roast** — A trivia game where an AI host asks questions AND roasts players for wrong answers. Customizable categories, difficulty, and roast intensity.

**Fake Artist** — A drawing bluffing game. Everyone gets a secret word to draw — except the fake artist, who must bluff. AI helps generate words and judge the drawings.

**The Whispering House** — A multiplayer mystery game set in a haunted house. AI generates the mystery, assigns roles, and each player uncovers clues to solve it together.

All Arcade games work on any device with a browser. No app download required. Players join via room codes or QR codes.

## Privacy & Data Ownership

Magic Bus Studios is built with privacy at the center:

- **Your data stays in your apps.** Deleting your data from one product doesn't affect any other product. Only you decide what to keep and what to remove.
- **Inner Lab sharing is opt-in.** Cross-module insights only flow when you explicitly allow them. By default, each module's AI only knows what you've shared within that module.
- **Pseudonymous access available.** Nostr and Lightning wallet users can use the platform without providing an email address.
- **Full account control.** Delete individual app data, an entire product line's data, or your complete account at any time from your account settings.

## Technology Philosophy

Every product in the MBS ecosystem is:
- **Web-first** — Works in any browser, on any device. No app store required.
- **AI-powered** — OpenAI drives the intelligence behind every product, from spiritual guidance to recipe generation to game hosting.
- **Dark-themed** — Premium visual design with glass morphism, smooth animations, and a consistent aesthetic across the ecosystem.
- **Self-hosted** — The entire infrastructure runs on our own servers, not on third-party cloud platforms. Your data never passes through AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure.
