## What Changed: MBS Now Has a Unified Platform

MagicBusStudios is building a centralized platform that unifies identity, billing, and entitlements across ALL 22 products. This affects how we talk about the user experience.

### Key messaging updates:

**One Account, Everything:**
- Users sign up ONCE and get access to any MBS product they purchase
- One login works across Inner Lab modules, Arcade games, and Studio Works tools
- No separate accounts per product — one profile, one billing portal

**Inner Lab Is a Connected System (reinforced):**
- Inner Lab modules share consciousness data, memories, and insights across modules
- What you learn in CWG informs your BreathArc recommendations
- Your yoga practice in FlowState connects to your spiritual journey in CWG
- This is the technical reality now, not just positioning — the middleware exists

**Tiered Access Model:**
- Individual module access (e.g., just CWG) — standalone experience
- Inner Lab All Access — all 11 modules + unified dashboard + cross-module intelligence
- MBS All Access — everything across all three product lines

**Data Sovereignty Is Real:**
- Users own their data
- Support for Nostr identity (decentralized, user-controlled)
- Lightning payments via BTCPay (Bitcoin-native, no intermediary)
- Client-side encryption available
- GDPR/CCPA compliance built into the platform
- This is a genuine differentiator, not just a claim

## Inner Lab Product Brief — Supplements

### Cross-Module Intelligence (NEW — not in existing brief)
Inner Lab modules don't just share a login. They share understanding:
- **Consciousness Profile**: Your spiritual assessment from CWG informs recommendations across all modules
- **User Memories**: Facts the AI learns about you (with your permission) carry across modules
- **Daily Check-ins**: Mood and energy data from any module personalizes the others
- **Wellness Profile**: Health conditions and injuries from FlowState inform BreathArc safety filters
- **Activity Feed**: See your journey across all Inner Lab modules in one place

- Memories are private to each module by default
- Users explicitly opt-in to share data across modules
- Inner Lab deals with deeply personal/spiritual data — sovereignty is a feature, not a checkbox

### Inner Lab Dashboard (Coming)

- Daily briefing synthesizing insights from all modules
- Unified consciousness profile

## CWG Product Brief — Supplements

### Authentication (Updated)
- CWG login now goes through the MBS Platform (branded as Inner Lab)
- Three login methods: Google SSO, Nostr identity, Lightning wallet (LNURL)
- Users signing up from CWG see Inner Lab branding — they know they're joining a bigger ecosystem

### Subscription (Updated)
- CWG subscriptions managed centrally through MBS Platform
- Pricing: Free tier (5 messages/day) + Premium ($9.99/mo or $79.99/yr)
- Inner Lab All Access ($19.99/mo) unlocks CWG + all other modules + cross-module intelligence
- Lightning/Bitcoin payments available as alternative to Stripe

### Data Sovereignty (NEW — CWG-specific)
CWG has pioneered data sovereignty features for Inner Lab:
- Nostr identity (npub) — decentralized, user-owned identity
- Lightning payments — Bitcoin-native, no credit card required
- Client-side encryption — user holds the keys
- Blockchain anchors (OpenTimestamps) — verifiable data integrity
- GDPR-compliant data export and deletion

## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
- Frame Inner Lab as ONE system (existing guidance, reinforced by actual shared data now)
- Mention data sovereignty when talking about Inner Lab (it's real infrastructure, not marketing)
- Highlight the tiered access model (individual modules vs Inner Lab All Access)
- Position cross-module intelligence as a reason to upgrade from single module to All Access

### DON'T:
- Don't mention "MBS Platform" to users — they see "MagicBusStudios" or "Inner Lab" branding
- Don't describe the technical architecture — talk about the user experience
- Don't promise cross-module features that aren't built yet (daily briefing, insights are coming)
- Don't say "SSO" or "middleware" — say "one account" or "connected experience"
