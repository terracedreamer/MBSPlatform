## Platform Context (New)
- MBS now has a centralized platform at magicbusstudios.com that handles SSO, billing, and entitlements for ALL 22 products
- magicbusstudios.com serves as both the marketing site (public) AND the platform (login, billing, account management)
- One account works across all products -- users sign up once
- Three auth methods: Google SSO, Nostr identity, LNURL (Lightning wallet login)
- Payments: Stripe + BTCPay (Lightning) -- centralized, no per-product payment handling
- Branded login: Inner Lab modules show Inner Lab branding at login; Arcade/Studio Works show MBS branding
- Data sovereignty: users own their data. Nostr identity, Lightning payments, client-side encryption, GDPR/CCPA compliance
- Three-layer architecture:
  - Layer 1 (magicbusstudios.com): SSO + billing + entitlements + friends + push notifications + feature flags
  - Layer 2 (innerlab.ai): Shared consciousness data + user memories + daily briefing + Inner Lab dashboard
  - Layer 3: Individual products (each module/game/tool has own backend)
- Tiered subscriptions: product_pass (single product), category_access (all in a category), mbs_all_access (everything)
- Friends and invites are platform-level -- friendships carry across products

## 2. Mission, Vision & Philosophy

### Mission

Build technology that supports inner growth -- reflection, practice, and self-awareness. We create tools that help people observe themselves, understand patterns, take meaningful action, and build awareness over time.

### Vision

Make tools for self-discovery accessible, sovereign, and adaptable to every individual. We don't prescribe one path. We build modular, belief-inclusive tools that meet people where they are.

### Philosophy

We believe that self-discovery should be personal, private, and empowering. Technology should serve the soul -- not extract from it. Our tools are designed to give users something meaningful to take with them when they step away from the screen.

## 3. Core Values

### 1. You Should Own Your Growth
Your reflections, your patterns, your progress belong to you. Tools for self-discovery should be sovereign, not extractive. We build with privacy and data ownership as foundational principles.

### 2. One Path Doesn't Fit All
We don't prescribe a single method or belief system. Inner Lab brings together many modalities -- from dialogue to breathwork to astrology -- so you can find what resonates and leave the rest.

### 3. Small Moments Create Real Change
You don't need hour-long sessions. A few minutes of focused attention, consistently, can shift how you see yourself and the world. Our tools are designed for brief, meaningful daily engagement.

### 4. Technology Should Serve the Soul
We build software, but the goal isn't more screen time. It's to give you something meaningful to take with you when you step away. Every feature is measured by its impact on the user's inner life, not engagement metrics.

Individuals seeking tools for personal growth, self-reflection, and mindfulness. People who want structured support for inner work but don't necessarily identify with any one spiritual tradition.

### Secondary Audience
Spiritually curious people who want structured practice without dogma. They may explore astrology, tarot, breathwork, or meditation but want a non-prescriptive, inclusive environment.

### Tertiary Audience
Wellness-oriented professionals (therapists, coaches, yoga instructors) looking for supplementary digital tools to recommend to clients or integrate into their practice.

### Demographics
- **Age**: 25-45 years old
- Tech-savvy and comfortable with digital tools
- Values-driven and open-minded across all belief systems
- Global audience, English-primary (CWG supports 36 languages)

### Psychographics
- Seeks meaning and purpose in daily life
- Values privacy and sovereignty over personal data
- Prefers quality over quantity in tools and experiences
- Open to multiple modalities without rigid adherence to one system
- Appreciates design quality and premium user experience

## 12. User Flow Between Products

Users can enter the ecosystem from multiple points. The platform's unified SSO means one account works everywhere.

### Flow A: Studio-First Discovery
1. User discovers MagicBusStudios (search, social, referral)
2. Learns about the studio, its values, what it builds
3. Clicks "Explore Inner Lab" -- arrives at innerlab.ai
4. Understands the unified system, browses modules
5. Clicks on CWG or FlowState -- tries a live module
6. Has meaningful experience -- wants more modules
7. Joins waitlist on MBS, subscribes to updates, becomes advocate

### Flow B: Product-First Discovery
1. User discovers CWG directly (search, ad, recommendation)
2. Uses CWG -- has meaningful conversations, tries meditation/journaling
3. Learns CWG is part of Inner Lab -- visits innerlab.ai
4. Sees other modules (breathwork, astrology, dreams) -- gets excited
5. Joins waitlist for coming-soon modules
6. Discovers MagicBusStudios -- sees Studio Works, The Arcade
7. Understands the full creative studio and its mission

### Flow C: Utility Discovery
1. User finds a Studio Works app (Movie Picker, Task Tracker, etc.)
2. Notices it's by MagicBusStudios -- visits the studio site
3. Discovers Inner Lab -- resonates with the mission
4. Tries CWG or FlowState -- begins inner growth journey
